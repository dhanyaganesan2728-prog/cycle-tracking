About MyCycle

MyCycle started as a response to gaps in existing trackers — complexity, poor privacy, and limited wellness support. The approach below is from your submission. :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}

Approach

  1. User Research — Understand needs across ages 13–45.
  2. Core Features — Tracking, symptom logging, AI predictions.
  3. Mascot & Engagement — Friendly messages and gamification.
  4. Privacy — Secret mode & encryption for data.

Outcomes

  • Simplified cycle management
  • Reduced stress via wellness recommendations
  • Higher user engagement with gamification
